there are three artists that inspired me

Alex Ross

He is a American Comic Book Artist famous for his covers and design works. He inspiring to me because he balances superheroes with stunning realism that breathe new life into those iconic heroes and he manages to achieve his dream of drawing his favorite heroes which let to win many awards showing us that you too can achieve your dream if you put effort into it.

Hirohiko Araki

The Japanese manga artist himself Hirohiko Araki is well known for his Jojo Bizarre Adventure series. He's inspire me because his williness to keep on reinventing himself and dedication to his work shows that everyone can create something great as long as they're dedicated to what they're work on.

Bryan Lee O'Mallery

A Canadian Cartoonist best known for creating the Scott Pilgrim Series and was a musician artist under the name Kupek. Bryan Lee O'Mallery inspire me due to evolving his artstyle to expends his art horizon and maintaining his unique style and artistic voice when he bring his iconic series to the big screen.
